Settings

การตั้งค่า gaan dtâng kâa

Display Choose what's shown on the Thai-language cards: both Thai script and romanization (default), just the Thai script, or just the romanization.
Speed Determines how long unmatched cards stay flipped before turning back over. Slower speed gives you more time to read and memorize them. Also affects how quickly CPU opponents take their turns.
After Matching a Pair What happens to matched pairs. "Keep visible" leaves them on the board. "Remove cards" makes them disappear.
In-Game Popups Controls in-game popups. On shows everything — celebratory streak popups and messages from CPU players. Streak keeps the streak popups but silences CPU chat. Off hides both for a calmer, less distracting game.
Card Back Choose the artwork shown on the back of each card during games.
